THE WORLD

By 2071, physical passports no longer exist. The Global Mobility Authority — a private-public coalition born from the climate crisis agreements of 2041 — replaced all national border systems with a single algorithmic score: the Mobility Clearance Score (MCS).

WHO CONTROLS THE BORDER

No single government does. The GMA is controlled by a board of 12 corporate signatories and 4 surviving climate bloc nations. Borders are AI-enforced at biometric checkpoints. There are no human guards — only sensor gates and automated detainment.

HOW PEOPLE CROSS

A retinal scan at any checkpoint reads your embedded UID chip. The MovePass app updates in real-time. Scores above 850 move freely. Scores below 500 are flagged for "mobility suspension" — effectively house arrest within an 80km zone.

WHAT NO LONGER EXISTS

Passports. Visas. Asylum law. The 1951 Refugee Convention was suspended in 2048 and never reinstated. The concept of citizenship has been replaced by tier classification. You don't belong to a country — you belong to a score bracket.

NEW SYSTEM

The MCS algorithm. It tracks employment, biometric compliance, zone history, energy consumption, and social credit data from 47 partner platforms. Your score is continuously updated. It cannot be fully audited — the weighting model is proprietary.
